About Us

Welcome To Kanunga Extrusion Pvt Ltd (KEPL)

Your Trusted JSW Steel Distributor and Partner in Growth. Established in 1979, KEPL is a leading force in the steel distribution industry, delivering Quality Products and unparalleled service to customers in South India. Our journey, initiated by our Group Chairman Mr. Ranjeet Kanunga, was started with a vision to redefine steel distribution. Over the years, under the able leadership of his Son Mr. Jitu Kanunga, we've evolved into a dynamic firm known for being a Reliable Partner, Dependability, and a Customer-Centric Approach

KEPL's 45-year journey began with addressing the steel requirements of Various Industries in Bangalore. Today, we proudly stand as the preferred partner for businesses across various industries in South India. As a distinguished Steel Dealership for JSW Steel, KEPL has expanded its expertise in sourcing materials globally, collaborating with over 12 different mills worldwide, and delivering a comprehensive range of steel products. Our commitment to Global Sourcing, providing Diverse Steel Products, and maintaining the highest standards of Quality and Trust has made KEPL synonymous with excellence in the steel industry.

Our Ethos

Customer-Centric Approach

At KEPL, ensuring Customer Delight is our foremost priority. We are dedicated to build lasting relationships by comprehending our client's unique requirements and providing tailored solutions. We offer raw materials in various forms, including Slit Coils, Sheets, Strips, and cut-to-length Blanks, to align with our customer's production needs. Our committed team of professionals strives to deliver personalized service, prompt deliveries, On time In Full, high quality products, all required products in the necessary quantity, along with suitable credit options, and transparent communication throughout the entire process.

Quality Assurance

At KEPL, being an ISO 9001 certified company, we prioritize quality along with unmatched service. Understanding the critical role steel plays in various industries, we adhere to the highest industry standards to ensure our products and services exceed expectations. Our stringent quality control measures guarantee the durability and reliability of every piece of steel that leaves our facilities, reinforcing our dedication to delivering excellence in the form of high-quality, high-performance steel products.

Products

Comprehensive Product Range

KEPL provides a wide range of steel products to meet diverse customer needs.

KEPL provides a wide range of steel Grades to meet diverse customer needs. Our inventory includes Hot Rolled Steel grades like E 250, E 350, SS 400, Fe 410, Fe 490, and BSK 46, along with HRPO options such as IS 1079 D, SAPH 440, SPFH 590, and BSK 46. We offer Cold Rolled options, covering base grades like IS 513 D, EDD, and special grades like SPFC 590, JSC 440. Our stainless steel selection includes grades from the 200/300/400 series for various applications. GP Coils are available in different grades and spangle types, while our structural steel range comprises products like MS Angle, MS channels, ISMC, ISMB, MS flats, and MS rods. Whether you need CRCA, HR, HRPO, GP, Aluminum, Stainless Steel or Structural Steel, KEPL ensures a comprehensive inventory to meet your specific project and product requirements.
